# Example Company Policy

This document is a sample source used by the RAG chatbot.

## Remote Work

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week after approval from their manager.

## Vacation

Employees should submit vacation requests at least two weeks in advance.

## Expenses

Business expenses must be submitted with receipts within 30 days.

## Learning Budget

Employees may use an annual learning budget for courses, books and conferences related to their role.

## Sick Leave

Employees should inform their manager as soon as possible when they are unable to work due to illness.
